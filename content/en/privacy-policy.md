### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on Our Service and store certain info. The tracking technologies used include beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze Our Service. The tech we use might include: 🍪

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file placed on Your Device. You can tell Your browser to refuse all Cookies or to let You know when one is being sent. But hey, if You don't accept Cookies, You might not be able to use certain parts of Our Service. Unless You've tweaked Your browser settings to reject 'em, Our Service might just use Cookies anyway. 🤷‍♀️
- **Web Beacons**: Certain parts of Our Service and Our emails might contain tiny electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company, for instance, count users who've visited specific pages or opened an email, and help with other website stats (like checking a section's popularity or verifying system integrity). Kinda cool, right? 😎

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on Your personal computer or mobile device even after You go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment You close Your web browser. Poof! 💨

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons: 👇

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super essential to give You the services available through the Website and to let You use some of its cool features. They help us authenticate users and stop any funky, fraudulent use of user accounts. Seriously, without 'em, the services You've asked for just can't happen, and We only use these Cookies for that sole purpose. They're the backbone! 💪

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about figuring out if You've given the green light to use Cookies on the Website. Simple as that! ✅

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let Us remember the choices You make when rocking the Website, like Your login deets or language preference. The whole point? To give You a more personalized experience, so You don't have to re-enter Your preferences every single time You visit. How convenient is that? ✨

Wanna know more about the Cookies We use and Your options regarding 'em? Hit up Our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of Our Privacy Policy. We've got the deets! 📖
